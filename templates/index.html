AI Game World Builder

Forge detailed lore, procedural geography, and high-fidelity concept art from a single vision. Explore the internal architecture of world-building through AI.

Aethel Pipeline Components

🌍 World Mind

01

Establishes the core metaphysical foundation, tone, and the primary protagonist of the universe.

INPUT: User prompt / Theme
OUTPUT: Theme, Tagline, Environment, Protagonist Profile

🗺️ Cartography

02

Generates geographical distribution, climate zones, and a custom procedural SVG coordinate map.

INPUT: Environment Tone
OUTPUT: Continents, Major Cities, Natural Wonders, SVG Map

🏛️ Societies

03

Populates the world with diverse civilizations, defining their unique races, cultures, and visual traits.

INPUT: World Geography
OUTPUT: Race details, Cultural descriptors, Visual aesthetics

⚔️ Factions

04

Maps the political landscape. Defines major powers, their leaders, and their interactive relationships.

INPUT: Civilizations
OUTPUT: Faction names, IDEOLOGY, Leaders, Relationship Web

📜 Chronicles

05

Compiles creation myths, ancient prophecies, and a linear timeline of major historical eras.

INPUT: Faction History
OUTPUT: Mythology, Legends, Villains, Prophecy, Eras

📋 Adventures

06

Constructs dynamic questlines with objectives, difficulty tiers, and narrative twists.

INPUT: World Lore & Politics
OUTPUT: Quests, Objectives, Difficulty, Rewards, Narrative Twists

🎮 Blueprints

07

Transposes narrative quests into mechanical level design logic and obstacle placement.

INPUT: Selected Quest
OUTPUT: Layout, Enemies, Obstacles, Collectibles, Win/Loss Conditions

🎨 Visions

08

Crafts high-fidelity concept art using NVIDIA NIM (SD3) based on AI-refined image prompts.

INPUT: Element Descriptions
OUTPUT: Character Portraits, Scene Renders, Asset Art
